My Memory Vault
Your personal, always-evolving knowledge base

My Memory Vault is a personal space to capture, organise, and revisit what you learn every day — grouped by category and topic so nothing gets lost. It is designed to grow with you: start simple today, and connect it to every tool you already use.

Access anywhere
📱
Mobile App
Native app for iOS & Android — capture learnings on the go, browse by category, and review past entries offline.
🌐
Web App
Full-featured browser experience — the same journal accessible from any desktop or laptop without installing anything.
💬
WhatsApp
Send a quick message to add a learning on the spot. Ask the bot to show you a topic and it replies with your notes.
🤖
ChatGPT & Claude
Custom MCP servers let you add or query your journal directly inside ChatGPT or Claude — no copy-paste needed.
Personalised for you
🔐
Mobile OTP login
Sign in with your mobile number and a one-time password — no passwords to remember, works on any device.
☁️
Your data, your journal
Every category, topic, and entry belongs to your account. Learnings are private and synced across all your devices.
On the roadmap
  • Cloud database — entries will move from a local Markdown file to a proper database, enabling real-time sync and search.
  • MCP server integrations — connect your journal to ChatGPT and Claude as a custom tool so AI assistants can read and write your learnings during any conversation.
  • WhatsApp bot — send a voice note or message to log a learning instantly; reply with a topic name to get a summary back.
  • Mobile app — iOS and Android apps with offline support, push reminders, and a quick-capture widget.
  • Search & smart review — full-text search across all entries and spaced-repetition prompts to help knowledge stick.
